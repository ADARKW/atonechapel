Our Mandate

Atone Bible School exists to equip believers with sound doctrine, spiritual authority, and practical ministry skills. We are committed to raising kingdom leaders who walk in holiness, demonstrate power, minister healing, and advance the gospel with excellence.

Core Pillars

Excellence in Leadership

Developing disciplined, accountable, and Spirit-led leaders who represent Christ with integrity and excellence.

Power & Spiritual Authority

Training believers to walk in dominion, prayer mastery, prophetic accuracy, and spiritual warfare.

Healing & Deliverance

Imparting biblical understanding and practical activation of divine healing, deliverance, and restoration.

Evangelism & Missions

Equipping students to preach the gospel boldly, win souls, disciple nations, and impact communities.

Training Curriculum

  • Foundations of Christian Doctrine & Sound Theology
  • Prayer, Intercession & Spiritual Warfare
  • Prophetic Ministry & Hearing the Voice of God
  • Healing, Deliverance & Power Ministry
  • Leadership, Character & Ministry Ethics
  • Evangelism, Missions & Church Planting
  • Practical Ministry Activation & Mentorship
